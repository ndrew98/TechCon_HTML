History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 50 technology enthusiasts in a modest conference room in Silicon Valley. What started as an intimate meetup has since transformed into a global technology conference that attracts over 5,000 attendees from around the world.

Over the past decade, we've been at the forefront of technological innovation, providing a platform for groundbreaking ideas and connecting the brightest minds in the tech industry.

Mission

Our mission is to create a dynamic environment that fosters innovation, facilitates knowledge sharing, and accelerates technological advancement. We believe in bringing together diverse perspectives to solve complex technological challenges and shape the future of our digital world.

TechCon is committed to:

  • Promoting cutting-edge technological research
  • Providing networking opportunities for professionals
  • Inspiring the next generation of tech innovators

Past Speakers

Dr. Elena Rodriguez

Dr. Rodriguez is a world-renowned AI researcher who has made significant contributions to machine learning and neural network technologies. Her keynote speech in 2022 on ethical AI development was a conference highlight.

Michael Chang

As the founder of SecureNet Solutions, Michael Chang has been a leading voice in cybersecurity. His workshop on emerging cybersecurity threats in 2021 was attended by industry leaders from around the globe.

Sarah Thompson

Sarah Thompson is a pioneer in quantum computing research. Her groundbreaking work has been instrumental in advancing quantum technology, and she has been a recurring speaker at TechCon since 2018.
